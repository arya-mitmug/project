What Your Local Government Can Do

Local Governments, who know the wants and needs of the people and the environment, play a big role in the green and sustainable development of cities and local areas. It is up to us to promote and advocate for our local governments to do the right thing. Here are some things that can be implemented by local governments.

  1. Improve Zoning and Land-use Regulations
  2. Develop a Clear Sustainability Vision and Policy Framework
  3. Promote Smart and Inclusive Land Use Planning
  4. Invest in Green Infrastrcture and Nature-Base Solutions
  5. Organize Green Community Events and Activities
